ABOUT INSTITUTION

The Jawahar degree college is affiliated to Gulbarga University,Gulbarga and it was established in the year 2001. The main objective of the Jawahar degree college is to provide education and to mold the students for leadership in industry, government, and educational institutions, to advance the knowledge base of the professions, and to influence the future directions of education and practice. Our primary concept is Design Thinking, where this approach to education provides the students to have the academic freedom to understand complex problems and principles through engaged learning activities and helps nurture progressive minds.

The educational programs in the college emphasize the understanding of fundamental principles and prepare an individual for a lifetime of learning and professional growth. With leading-edge faculty and remarkable alumni, we are committed to provide students with a challenging and relevant education for developing them to have an impact on the world and solve the most pressing social issues of our nation.
